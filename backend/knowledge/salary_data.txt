# Tech Startup Salary Benchmarks (2024-2025)

## Engineering & Development
- Software Engineer (USA/San Francisco): $130,000 - $180,000/yr
- Software Engineer (USA/Remote): $110,000 - $150,000/yr
- Software Engineer (Europe/London): £70,000 - £100,000/yr ($90k - $130k)
- Software Engineer (India/Bangalore): ₹1,500,000 - ₹3,000,000/yr ($18k - $36k)
- CTO / Lead Developer (USA): $160,000 - $220,000/yr + significant equity

## Product & Design
- Product Manager (USA): $120,000 - $160,000/yr
- UX/UI Designer (USA): $100,000 - $140,000/yr
- Product Manager (Europe): $80,000 - $120,000/yr

## Marketing & Sales
- Growth Hacker / Marketing Lead (USA): $100,000 - $150,000/yr
- Sales Executive (USA): $80,000 base + commission (OTE $150k+)
- Social Media Manager (Remote Global): $50,000 - $80,000/yr

## Operations & Finance
- Operations Manager (USA): $90,000 - $130,000/yr
- Startup CFO / VP Finance (USA): $150,000 - $200,000/yr

Note: Early stage startups often pay 10-20% below these market rates but compensate with 0.5% - 2% equity grants.
